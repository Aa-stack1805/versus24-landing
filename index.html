What makes Versus different

Built for the
hybrid athlete.

01
Core Feature

Technique Skill Tree

Map your progress through 14 martial arts curricula — BJJ, Muay Thai, Boxing, Wrestling, Judo, Sambo, Karate, and more. Techniques unlock as you train them, with a visual dependency graph showing exactly what opens next.

02
MAX Feature

AI Coach — Sensei

A coach that actually knows your history. Sensei reads your recent sessions, active training camp, fight record, and readiness before giving advice — so the guidance fits your actual situation, not a generic template.

03

Daily Readiness Score

One number every morning. Pulled from your HRV, sleep quality, and training load — calibrated to your own baseline, not a population average. Free for every user, every day.

04

Training Camps & Fight Week

Build a periodised training block with auto-generated deload, taper, and peak weeks — then switch into Fight Week mode for weight tracking, water intake, sauna logging, and a countdown to weigh-in.

05

Gym Feed

Join your gym with a code and see who trained today, react to sessions, and stay accountable together. Training is more consistent when the people around you can see the work.

06

Vanity Vault

Progress photos, 12 body measurements, automatic body fat estimation via the Navy Method, and a before/after comparison view. Because looking the part is part of the goal too.
