What makes Versus different

Built for the
hybrid athlete.

01

Technique Skill Tree

Map your progress through 14 martial arts curricula — BJJ, Muay Thai, Boxing, Wrestling, Judo, Sambo, Karate, Lethwei, Capoeira and more. Techniques unlock as you train them, with a visual dependency graph showing what opens next.

02

Daily Readiness Score

A single number every morning telling you whether to push or recover. Calculated from your HRV, sleep quality, and training load — calibrated to your own baseline, not a population average. Free for every user, every day.

03

Gym Feed

Join your gym with a code and see who trained today, react to sessions, and stay accountable together. Training is more consistent when the people around you can see the work you're putting in.

04

AI Coach — Sensei

A coach that actually knows your history. Sensei reads your recent sessions, active training camp, fight record, and readiness before giving advice — so the guidance fits your actual situation, not a generic template.

05

Training Camps and Fight Week

Build a periodised training block with auto-generated deload, taper, and peak weeks — then when competition approaches, switch into Fight Week mode for daily weight tracking, water intake, sauna logging, and a countdown to weigh-in.

06

Vanity Vault

Track the physique side of training. Progress photos, 12 body measurements, automatic body fat estimation via the Navy Method, and a before/after comparison view. Because looking the part is part of the goal too.
