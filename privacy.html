Policy

Applies to the Versus iOS application

Effective date: March 12, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Versus app (referred to as the "Application") for mobile devices, developed by Aadit Singal (referred to as the "Service Provider") as a freemium service. This service is provided "AS IS".

Information We Collect

The Application collects information you provide when you register and use it. This includes:

  • Email address
  • First and last name
  • Age (stored as approximate date of birth)
  • Gender

The Application also collects certain information automatically, including the type of mobile device you use, your device's unique ID, IP address, mobile operating system, browser type, and information about how you use the Application.

Health and Fitness Data

The Application reads health and fitness data from Apple HealthKit, including heart rate variability (HRV) and sleep data. This data is used solely to calculate your daily readiness score. Health data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than delivering the readiness features within the Application. All health data processing is performed in accordance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines and applicable privacy laws.

User-Generated Content

The Application allows users to submit progress photos and record body measurements through the Vanity Vault feature. This content is stored securely in the Service Provider's cloud infrastructure and is used solely to display your own progress within the Application. Progress photos and body measurements are never shared with third parties, sold, or used for advertising purposes.

Artificial Intelligence

The Application uses AI technologies to enhance user experience through the Sensei coaching feature. AI components may process your training history, readiness data, session logs, and fight records to generate personalised coaching advice, session plans, and sparring analysis. Specifically:

  • Personalised coaching responses based on your logged sessions and readiness score
  • AI-generated daily session recommendations based on your training history
  • Sparring debrief analysis based on post-session input
  • Opponent scouting and gameplan suggestions based on structured profile data

All AI outputs are recommendations the user acts on voluntarily. No automated decisions with legal or significant personal effect are made by the Application. All AI processing is performed in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable laws.

Location Data

The Application does not collect precise or approximate location information from your device. The Application does not request location permissions.

Third Party Access

Only aggregated, anonymised data is periodically transmitted to external services to help improve the Application. The Service Provider may share your information with third parties as described in this policy.

The Application uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and cloud storage
  • RevenueCat — subscription and payment management
  • Sentry — error tracking and analytics
  • PostHog — product analytics
  • Apple HealthKit — health and fitness data

The Service Provider may disclose your information:

  • As required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process
  • When disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others, or to investigate fraud
  • With trusted service providers who work on our behalf, who have no independent use of the information disclosed to them, and who have agreed to adhere to this privacy policy

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the Application by uninstalling it using the standard uninstall processes available on your device or via the App Store.

Data Retention

The Service Provider will retain user-provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter. Automatically collected information is retained for up to 24 months and may thereafter be stored in aggregate form. If you would like your user data deleted, please contact us at aadit@versus24.net and we will respond within a reasonable time. Note that some data may be required for the Application to function properly.

Children's Privacy

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, it will be immediately deleted from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at aadit@versus24.net.

Security

The Service Provider takes the confidentiality of your information seriously and provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect the information we process and maintain. Access to your information is limited to authorised employees and contractors who need it to operate, develop, or improve the Application. Please be aware that no security system can prevent all potential breaches.

GDPR Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to or restrict processing of your data
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at aadit@versus24.net.
